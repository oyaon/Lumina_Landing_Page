Lumina Core
A New Architecture

Built on a
different premise

Every other tool asks you to organize your work. Lumina asks a harder question: what does your work actually mean? Then it builds the connections for you.

01

Ideas, not documents

In Lumina, every concept is a living object — versioned, linked, and aware of what it relates to. Not a file. Not a note. A node in your team's growing understanding.

02

Inference over search

You shouldn't have to remember where you put something to use it. Lumina reasons across your workspace and surfaces what you need — before you know you need it.

03

Memory for long-horizon work

Projects that span years lose coherence in every other tool. Lumina holds the thread — so six months from now, the context is still there, still sharp, still useful.
